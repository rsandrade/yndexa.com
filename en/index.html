Farinha

Free software for institutions with permanent archives and memorials.

Developed to facilitate the management and access of cultural, scientific, and historical collections with technical precision, allowing institutions and other entities to preserve and share their documentary heritage in an accessible and robust way.

  • Easy deployment: one installation, many institutions
  • Multi-institutional: with description via the ISDIAH standard
  • Multi-sectoral: reflects the organizational complexity of any institution
  • Multi-collections: funds described with NOBRADE and collections with custom metadata
  • Autonomous teams: granular permissions per team and workflow
  • Digital objects: images, audio, and audiovisual integrated into the collection
  • Advanced search: with weights, navigation through structure, and natural language with AI (coming soon)
  • Map: georeferencing of institutions and sectors
  • Free source code: digital sovereignty and technical sustainability

Built with Python, Django, Docker, SQLite and Bulma. Development team based in Salvador, Bahia, Brazil.

About

A company founded for
technology, information and culture.

Yndexa Tecnologia e Informação works at the intersection of archival science and the technologies that allow this work to scale with rigor and intelligence.

We believe that good free tools strengthen institutions, democratize access to information, and preserve collective memory.

Services

What we do

Farinha — deployment, training and support

Installation and configuration of Farinha in the institution's infrastructure, team training for system use, and ongoing support throughout the platform's lifecycle.

Consulting for permanent archives

Support for the implementation of permanent archives — funds and collections — with technical guidance for organizing and describing physical and digital documentation according to archival standards (NOBRADE, ISAD-G).
